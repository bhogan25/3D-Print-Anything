Welcome to my Blog

For the past couple of years I've been tinkering with my Ender 3 printer and making wierd models in Onshape and I wanted a place to organize all my files. I also happened to need an idea for my final project for a computer science course, which lead to the creation of this blog. Its nothing fancy, mostly a project where I could test my skills at creating and implementing something unique, applicable to me and my interests, and utilizing technology with which I was not completely familiar with at the time. I have thoroughly enjoyed the process of creating it and maybe one day, with a lot more work, I'll launch it for everyone to use and enjoy.
