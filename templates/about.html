About the Project

Allow me to introduce myself, my name is Ben Hogan and this is my final project for CS50x. The goal for this project was to challenge myself and create something that was unique to my interests while integrating the skills I leaned through CS50x. I love 3D printing because it gives anyone with the desire to learn a way to design and create tangible things that would otherwise cost thousands of dollars and years of training to produce. I use an Ender3 Pro FDM printer (picture above) and Unimaker's Cura for slicing software. For models I use the sites listed on the search page, and for designing my own modesl I use Onshape. All of which are free. Hopefully this will be only the first of many projects of this kind that I will be creating as my transition to web development ensues.
